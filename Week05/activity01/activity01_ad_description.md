### Activity Diagram: Restaurant Ingredient Ordering 

## Purpose: 
Manage ingredient inventory and procurement

## Actors: 
Ingredient Supply Manager (Primary), System, Restaurant Manager (Secondary)

## Precondition: 
System is functioning and equipped with the necessary data including the ingredients data

## Successful postcondition: 
Ingredients orders are confirmed and fulfilled

## Failed postcondition: 
Ingredients orders are not justifiable and the primary actor will be informed accordingly.
